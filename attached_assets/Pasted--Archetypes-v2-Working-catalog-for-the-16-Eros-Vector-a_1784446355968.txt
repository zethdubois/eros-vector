# Archetypes (v2)

Working catalog for the 16 Eros Vector archetypes.

These archetypes are **orientation patterns**, not identities, diagnoses, or prescriptions. Each names one orthant of the four-axis model. A person may recognize themselves in several descriptions, sit near a boundary, or move between orthants across Scouting, Bound, Past, Present, Aspiration, and Horizon contexts.

The names are memorable handles for the geometry. The coordinates and descriptions are the actual definition.

A **relational core** is a bonded group of two or more people around which significant commitment, priority, or shared life is organized. A **relational system** is the wider web of connections within and beyond any such core.

## Interpretation notes

- **Names are prototypes.** A person in Closed Builder may be monogamous, polyfidelitous, or part of another closed relational core.
- **Magnitude matters.** Someone barely inside an orthant may feel less represented by its name than someone near its outer corner.
- **Context matters.** Scouting and Bound modes may resolve to different archetypes at the same time point.
- **Time matters.** Movement between archetypes can be more informative than any single label.
- **Boundaries are continuous.** A score of zero is a genuine balance point, not a failure to classify.
- **Ethics are separate.** Consent, honesty, care, and agreement-keeping are not inferred from an orthant.
