**The Eros Vector: A 4D Relationship Mapping Tool**

Most relationship quizzes give you a static label—like "Swinger" or "Solo Poly"—and call it a day. The Eros Vector maps your trajectory.

Instead of forcing you into a 2D box, it plots your relational orientation across three diagnostic domains:

- **Architecture: Interdependent vs. Autonomous** (How do you build your life?)
- **Drive: Erotic vs. Emotional** (Why are you connecting?)
- **Method: Directed vs. Organic** (How does it actually happen?)

But we know people operate differently when single versus coupled. So the Eros Vector maps you in **Dual-Mode**: your "Scouting" pattern (how you seek and play when unbound) and your "Bound" architecture (how you operate when pair-bonded). Because it measures _orientation_ rather than just behavior, it accurately maps everyone—from strictly monogamous pair-bonds to fluid polycules—without making casual daters feel like they have to adopt a non-monogamous identity just because they're sleeping around.

Then comes the real innovation: the 4th axis—**Time**.

Using a "Sex-Age" metric, the survey dynamically adjusts. You define your own Past Eras (mapping both your Scouting and Bound histories), your Present, your Desired Next Step, and your Distant Horizon. By plotting these points in sequence, the Eros Vector generates a 4D trajectory. It reveals the shape of your relational life—showing loops, pivots, the "Dave" transition from casual dating to pair-bonding, and the friction gap between where you are and where you want to be.

It’s not just a diagnostic; it’s a compass for evolution. I'm building this out and would love your thoughts on the framework!
